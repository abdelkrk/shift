Privacy Policy  ·  Effective date: March 16, 2026

Overview

SHIFT is a single-player letter puzzle game. It does not require an account, does not collect your name, email, location, or any personally identifying information, and does not share your gameplay data with any third party — with the sole exception of the Google AdMob advertising SDK, described below.

This policy explains exactly what data is collected, by whom, and why.

Data We Collect

Collected by us
Game Progress (stored locally on your device)

SHIFT saves your puzzle progress — levels completed, stars earned, and your current level — on-device using Android's DataStore Preferences. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

On-device only Never transmitted No account required
Collected by Google (third party)
Advertising Data via Google AdMob

SHIFT displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use certain data from your device in order to serve personalised or contextual ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID) — a resettable device identifier
  • Approximate IP-derived location (country/region)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size, language)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • App usage signals used for ad targeting

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. We do not receive or store this data ourselves.

Collected by Google Not stored by us

What We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email address, or any contact information
  • Precise GPS or fine location data
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contacts, calendar, or files
  • Account credentials or payment information
  • Any data from children under 13 (see Children's Privacy below)

App Permissions

SHIFT requests only one device permission:

Permission
INTERNET

Required solely to load and display advertisements via Google AdMob. The app does not use this permission to transmit your gameplay data or any personal information.

Advertising

SHIFT uses three ad formats provided by Google AdMob:

  • Banner ads — displayed at the bottom of the home screen
  • Interstitial ads — shown occasionally between levels, at natural transition points
  • Rewarded interstitial ads — shown voluntarily when you choose to watch an ad in exchange for an in-game hint. You are always given the option to decline.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings: Settings › Google › Ads › Opt out of Ads Personalisation. You can also reset your Advertising ID from the same menu.

For more information on how Google uses data from apps, visit: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Children's Privacy

SHIFT is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Google AdMob is configured for general audiences; if you believe a child under 13 has been exposed to personalised advertising through this app, please contact us at the email below so we can take appropriate action.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with any personal data, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

The only data stored by the app is your local game progress, which remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear the app's data via Android Settings. We have no access to this data and cannot delete it on your behalf — you can remove it at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling SHIFT.

Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. Since we do not collect or store personal data ourselves, most of these rights apply to data held by Google rather than by us.

  • Opt out of personalised ads — via Android Settings › Google › Ads
  • Reset your Advertising ID — via Android Settings › Google › Ads
  • Delete local game data — via Android Settings › Apps › SHIFT › Clear Data
  • Requests regarding Google's data — myaccount.google.com

Security

Because SHIFT stores all data locally and does not transmit personal information to any server, there is no server-side data at risk of breach. Your game progress is protected by your device's standard Android security model.
